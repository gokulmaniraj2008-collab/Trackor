🌾 Uzhavan Farm

You are the only farmer. Grow crops from seed to harvest using your tractor, harvester, water and fertilizer!

1
Use the joystick (bottom-left) to walk around the farm
1b
Drag anywhere on the screen (away from the joystick and buttons) to freely look around — full 360° camera
2
Tap 🚜 TRACT. near the tractor to drive it over soil → tills the field
3
Stand on a tilled plot → tap 🌱 Seed to plant it instantly
4
Tap 💧 Water and 🧪 Fert. anytime near a growing plot to feed it — each tap acts right away (green flash = success, red = can't right now)
5
Watch plants grow: Seed→Sprout→Vegetative→Flowering→🍅 Ripe!
6
Drive 🌾 HARV. harvester over ripe crops to collect them
7
Visit the 🐔 Poultry Yard — tap Hand to collect eggs, tap Seed to feed the chickens (Kannan also collects eggs and Anitha feeds them automatically)
8
Visit the 🎣 Fishery by the pond — tap Hand to catch fish, tap Seed to feed them (Arun also catches fish and Bala feeds them automatically)
9
Your named farmhands work the crops, poultry, and fishery alongside you — Karthik tills, Velu plants, Muthu waters, Selvam fertilizes, and Raman harvests, one plot at a time. Water, fertilizer, and seeds are unlimited!
